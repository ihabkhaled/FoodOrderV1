# FoodOrderV1 v1.5.1

Fix invitation notifications, responsive layouts, scrolling, and navigation

## Highlights

- Friend-to-bucket sharing is now an explicit invitation: the recipient receives an actionable notification, access remains closed until acceptance, and the owner is notified after acceptance or decline.
- Friend-request and bucket activity notifications are deployed through the trusted v2 Functions notification path with idempotent records and ownership isolation.
- Bucket, order, and group screens now use document scrolling, responsive card actions, safe text wrapping, and narrow-screen layouts down to 304 px without clipped controls.
- Back controls preserve validated in-app origins, reset route scroll, and refresh same-route notification destinations.
- Playwright coverage now seeds long bucket/order/group content and checks desktop, tablet, mobile, scrolling, overflow, bottom navigation, and Back-route behavior.
