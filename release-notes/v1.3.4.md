# FoodOrderV1 v1.3.4

Bucket pricing now belongs to the bucket itself instead of the sharing workflow.

## Highlights

- Add VAT, service percentage, delivery amount, and split methods to bucket creation and editing.
- Apply bucket pricing to private single-person orders and show the full charge breakdown before and after placement.
- Preserve the complete pricing policy when duplicating a bucket.
- Remove pricing controls from the sharing page.
- Allow trusted personal pricing snapshots through Firestore Security Rules while keeping multi-person group receipts callable-only.
- Add unit, Security Rules, and Playwright regression coverage.

## Android APK

- File: FoodOrderV1-v1.3.4-debug.apk
- versionCode 5 / versionName 1.3.4
- SHA-256: filled automatically by the release workflow

## Known limitations

- Split methods affect multi-person buckets; for a one-person order all charges are assigned to that person.
