# Privacy promises

The app stores only identity profile fields, user-created buckets, user-created orders, and device preferences needed for the product. It does not require camera, microphone, location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or biometric processing. Data is not shared between users by product behavior.

Retention and account-wide deletion are not yet approved production promises. They must be finalized before launch.
