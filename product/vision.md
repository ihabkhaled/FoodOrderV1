# Vision

Make repeated group or personal food ordering faster than rebuilding the same list in chat or notes. The product prioritizes reusable structure, simple quantity selection, transparent totals, accessible mobile interaction, and ownership of personal data.

Success means a returning user can reach a previous bucket and place a new order in a small number of taps without losing historical context.
